Mini-Project

Wireless Power Transmission Using Solar Energy

Purpose of this project is to provide the power to the devices Without any physical contact of wire. This project works on the basic principle of electromagnetic induction. The projects consists of two coils one is called a transmission coil and the second is called a receiver coil. The power is converted into electromagnetic wave which is than received by the receiver and converted back into the power.
